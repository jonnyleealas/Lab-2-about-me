Get to know me

  My name is Jonathon Lee. When I'm asked about myself, I usually tell about having to move around most of my life. I've lived in several states and cities. I even I lived in El Salvador for a year when I was ten. Constantly moving helped me go to 15 schools before graduating high school! I sometimes refer to my family as a bunch of nomads; always moving around for a new place to live! However, I am thankful that I've experienced as much as I have. It's made me more adaptable.
  Another thing I can tell about who I am is that I like making people laugh. I'm constantly trying to find a way to find humor in things. It's kind of a rush when a bit actually works and the whole room is laughing. There are times when I end up looking dumb and no one laughs, but that's the risk that you take!
  Last thing I'll talk about are some of the things that I'm into. I'm in a band. We've played shows thoughout Wa, and even toured a bit in the past years. We are currently working on our first album; which we've been working on for 3 years. I'm also into cooking. One of my favorite things to cook are Carbonara or Cacio pepe. When learning a recipe I'll usually cook the same thing each day for a few weeks! It sounds weird to people when I tell them I've been eating Carbonara for the past two weeks; but, how else will I learn how to cook it correctly? Last thing I'll say is that I love sneakers and jeans. I won't get too much into that; it'll take too long!
